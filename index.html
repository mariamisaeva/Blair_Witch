About us

Blair Witch is a survival horror video game developed by Bloober Team based on the Blair Witch series of horror films.
It was published by Lionsgate Games for Microsoft Windows, Xbox One and Xbox Game Pass on August 30, 2019.
The game is set in 1996, two years after the events of The Blair Witch Project.
